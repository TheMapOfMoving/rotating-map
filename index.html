THE MAP OF MOVING

The Map of Moving is a community-based digital platform—a website working both for smartphones and desktops—where everyone is able to share and discover migration experiences of all kinds. We have the opportunity to tell our story, from the origin to the current location, with every stop in between, the reason for migrating, the time period, and the transport used. The goal is to share who we are, where we come from, and how our identity is shaped.

This project was born to address the loss of personal stories regarding migration in official reports. The goal is to act as a public archive of individual experiences that, until now, had nowhere to be expressed.

If you want to know more about the project, or want to send us some feedback, you can contact us here.
